About Me
Applied statistician with significant scientific computing experience. Currently working on a number of problems at the intersection of biology, computer science, and statistics. Also keen on researching, developing, and integrating project management frameworks to enhance bioinformatics analyst productivity and facilitate project reproducibility. In a former role as lead statistician in a high-throughput genomics lab, I was tasked with designing pipelines for conducting genome-wide association studies (GWAS) and implementing population genetics analyses using high-density SNP genotyping data. Additionally, I have significant experience developing, comparing and validating workflows for the analysis of whole-genome and whole-transcriptome next-generation sequencing data.
